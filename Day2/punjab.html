Explore the Vibrant Culture of Punjab

Welcome to Punjab

Punjab, the land of five rivers, is known for its rich culture, history, and the warmth of its people. From the iconic Golden Temple in Amritsar to the vibrant fields of the rural heartland, Punjab is a place that offers a deep connection to its heritage. With its beautiful cities, lively culture, and delicious cuisine, Punjab is the perfect destination for anyone seeking an immersive experience in the heart of India.

Major Attractions

Golden Temple

The holiest shrine of Sikhism located in Amritsar, known for its magnificent architecture and spiritual significance.

Qila Androon

Qila Androon, a majestic fort in the heart of Patiala, stands as a testament to the rich history and vibrant culture of Punjab.

Patiala Garden

A beautiful garden in Patiala known for its lush greenery and peaceful ambiance, perfect for relaxation.
