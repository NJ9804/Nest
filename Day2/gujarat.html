Explore the Beauty of Gujarat

Welcome to Gujarat

Gujarat, the land of legends, is a vibrant state filled with rich history, colorful traditions, and breathtaking landscapes. From the awe-inspiring Gir Forest to the majestic White Rann of Kutch, Gujarat offers diverse experiences for every traveler. Discover the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi, explore ancient temples, and indulge in mouth-watering Gujarati cuisine.

Major Attractions

Somnath Temple

A sacred pilgrimage site, Somnath Temple is a magnificent example of Hindu architecture along the Arabian Sea.

Rann of Kutch

The stunning white desert of Kutch comes alive during the Rann Utsav with culture, adventure, and art.

Sabarmati Ashram

The historical ashram of Mahatma Gandhi on the banks of the Sabarmati River reflects India's freedom struggle.
