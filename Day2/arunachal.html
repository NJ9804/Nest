Explore the Natural Beauty and Culture of Arunachal Pradesh

Welcome to Arunachal Pradesh

Arunachal Pradesh, often referred to as the "Land of the Dawn-Lit Mountains," is located in the northeastern part of India. Known for its pristine landscapes, rich culture, and diverse wildlife, this state offers an unforgettable experience for adventure lovers and nature enthusiasts. From the snow-capped mountains of Tawang to the serene valleys of Ziro, Arunachal Pradesh is a paradise for travelers seeking both tranquility and adventure.

Major Attractions

Tawang Monastery

Tawang Monastery is one of the largest Buddhist monasteries in India, perched high on the mountains and offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Ziro Valley

Known for its lush greenery, rice fields, and the unique Apatani tribal culture, Ziro Valley is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and culture enthusiasts.

Sela Pass

Located at an altitude of 13,700 feet, Sela Pass offers mesmerizing views of snow-covered peaks and is a popular spot for trekkers and adventure seekers.
