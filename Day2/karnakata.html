Explore the Rich Heritage of Karnataka

Welcome to Karnataka

Karnataka, known for its historical landmarks, serene landscapes, and vibrant culture, offers a unique experience for every traveler. From the majestic ruins of Hampi to the lush coffee plantations of Coorg, Karnataka is a land full of natural beauty and historical significance. Whether you're an adventure seeker or a history buff, Karnataka has something to offer everyone.

Major Attractions

Bangalore Palace

A grand palace in Bangalore, known for its Tudor-style architecture and beautiful interiors, offering a glimpse into the royal history of Karnataka.

Vijaya Vitthala Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage site located in Hampi, famous for its iconic stone chariot and intricate architecture that reflects the glory of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Coorg

A picturesque hill station known for its lush coffee plantations, waterfalls, and scenic landscapes, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers.
