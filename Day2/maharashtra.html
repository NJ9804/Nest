Explore the Beauty and Heritage of Maharashtra

Welcome to Maharashtra

Maharashtra, the heart of western India, is a land of diverse landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture. From the beautiful beaches of Goa to the majestic hill stations of Lonavala, Maharashtra offers a treasure trove of experiences for every traveler. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast, a history lover, or someone who enjoys a relaxing vacation, Maharashtra has it all.

Major Attractions

Gateway of India

A historic monument in Mumbai, symbolizing the grandeur of the British era and the gateway for travelers visiting India.

Ajanta Caves

A UNESCO World Heritage site, these ancient caves in Aurangabad are known for their stunning Buddhist murals and sculptures.

Mahabaleshwar

A scenic hill station famous for its strawberry farms, serene landscapes, and panoramic viewpoints, perfect for nature lovers.
